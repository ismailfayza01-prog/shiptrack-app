Frequently Asked Questions

Why is there a 20kg minimum?

The 20kg minimum billing ensures operational efficiency and fair pricing. Even if your shipment weighs less, you'll be charged for 20kg.

How do I qualify for B2B pricing?

Contact our sales team through the staff portal. B2B pricing requires business verification and minimum monthly volume commitments.

Can I mix relay pickup and home delivery?

Yes! Choose delivery method for each shipment. Home delivery incurs a MAD 5 fee, while relay pickup is free.

When do loyalty tokens expire?

Loyalty tokens never expire! Use them whenever you're ready for your next shipment.
